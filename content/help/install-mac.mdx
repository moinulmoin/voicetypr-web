## Download

1. Go to [voicetypr.com/download](/download)
2. Click **Download for Mac**
3. The `.dmg` file downloads to your Downloads folder

## Install

1. Open the `.dmg` file
2. Drag VoiceTypr to your Applications folder
3. Eject the disk image

## First Launch

macOS will warn you that VoiceTypr was downloaded from the internet. Click **Open**.

If you see "VoiceTypr can't be opened because it is from an unidentified developer":

1. Open **System Settings > Privacy & Security**
2. Scroll down to **Security**
3. Click **Open Anyway** next to VoiceTypr
4. Enter your password when prompted

## Grant Permissions

VoiceTypr needs two permissions to work:

### Microphone Access

1. Open **System Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone**
2. Toggle VoiceTypr **On**
3. Restart VoiceTypr

### Accessibility Access

This lets VoiceTypr paste text into other apps.

1. Open **System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility**
2. Click the **+** button
3. Navigate to **Applications > VoiceTypr**
4. Click **Open**
5. Toggle VoiceTypr **On**
