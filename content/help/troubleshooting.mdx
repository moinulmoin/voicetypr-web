## Microphone Not Working

### macOS

1. **Check System Permissions**
   - System Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone
   - Ensure VoiceTypr is toggled On
   - Restart VoiceTypr after granting permission

2. **Check Input Device**
   - System Settings > Sound > Input
   - Select your microphone
   - Test that the input level moves when you speak

3. **Try a Different Mic**
   - Built-in MacBook mic works best for most users
   - External USB mics may need driver updates

### Windows

1. **Check Privacy Settings**
   - Settings > Privacy > Microphone
   - Enable "Microphone access" and "Let apps access your microphone"
   - Find VoiceTypr and toggle On

2. **Check Default Device**
   - Right-click the speaker icon in taskbar
   - Select "Open Sound settings"
   - Verify correct input device is selected

## Slow or Laggy Transcription

**Cause**: Model too large for your RAM, or other apps using CPU.

**Fixes**:
- Switch to a smaller model (Whisper Base or Parakeet)
- Close unnecessary apps
- Ensure your Mac/PC meets the [model's RAM requirements](/help/models)

## Text Not Pasting

**macOS**: Grant Accessibility permission
- System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility
- Add VoiceTypr and toggle On

**Windows**: Run as administrator
- Right-click VoiceTypr > Run as administrator

## Crashes on Startup

1. Delete the model folder and re-download:
   - Mac: `~/Library/Application Support/voicetypr/models`
   - Windows: `%APPDATA%\voicetypr\models`
2. Restart VoiceTypr
3. Re-download your model

## Still Stuck?
