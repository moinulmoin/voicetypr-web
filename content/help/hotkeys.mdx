VoiceTypr's hotkey is how you trigger dictation without leaving the app you're working in.

## Default Hotkeys

| Platform | Default | Behavior |
|----------|---------|----------|
| macOS | Fn + F10 | Hold to record, release to stop |
| Windows | Ctrl + Shift + Space | Hold to record, release to stop |

## Changing Your Hotkey

1. Open VoiceTypr settings
2. Click **Hotkey**
3. Press your preferred combination
4. Click **Save**

Good hotkey choices:
- **Double-tap Ctrl** — Easy to reach, won't conflict
- **Fn + F12** — Unlikely used by other apps
- **Right Option** (Mac) — Dedicated key, no conflicts

## Tap vs Hold

### Hold to Record (Default)
- Press and hold while speaking
- Release to stop and paste text
- Best for: Short bursts, precise control

### Tap to Toggle
- Tap once to start recording
- Tap again to stop
- Best for: Long-form writing, walking around

To switch modes: **Settings > Hotkey > Toggle mode**

## Global Hotkeys

VoiceTypr registers your hotkey at the system level, so it works in any app:

- ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor
- VS Code, JetBrains, Vim
- Gmail, Notion, Slack
- Figma, Linear, Jira

If your hotkey doesn't work in a specific app, try running VoiceTypr as administrator (Windows) or check Accessibility permissions (Mac).
