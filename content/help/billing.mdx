## Free Trial

Every new user gets a **3-day free trial** with full access to all features.

- No credit card required
- No automatic charge when trial ends
- All models and features included

## Purchase Options

VoiceTypr is a **one-time purchase**. No subscription.

| Plan | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Personal** | $50 | 1 device, all features, lifetime updates |
| **Professional** | $90 | 3 devices, all features, lifetime updates |

Prices are in USD. VAT may apply depending on your country.

## Payment Methods

- Credit/debit card (via Stripe)
- Apple Pay (on Mac)
- Google Pay (on Windows)

## License Activation

1. Purchase from [voicetypr.com](/download)
2. You'll receive a license key via email
3. Open VoiceTypr > Settings > License
4. Enter your key and click Activate

## License Transfers

Moving to a new computer? You can deactivate your old device:

1. On old device: VoiceTypr > Settings > License > Deactivate
2. On new device: Install VoiceTypr and enter your key

**Personal license**: Transfer once per 30 days
**Professional license**: Transfer freely between your 3 devices

## Refunds

We offer a **30-day money-back guarantee**.

If VoiceTypr doesn't work for you:
1. Email [support@voicetypr.com](mailto:support@voicetypr.com)
2. Include your order number
3. Refund processed within 5 business days

No questions asked. We only ask that you tell us what didn't work so we can improve.
