## 1. Pick a Model

On first launch, VoiceTypr asks you to download a speech model.

**Recommended for beginners:**
- **Whisper Base** (~140 MB) — Fast, accurate for clear speech, 99+ languages
- **Whisper Large v3** (~2.9 GB) — Best accuracy, handles noise and accents better

The model downloads once and stays on your machine. No internet needed after that.

## 2. Set Your Hotkey

Default hotkey is **Fn + F10** (Mac) or **Ctrl + Shift + Space** (Windows).

To change it:

1. Open VoiceTypr settings
2. Click **Hotkey**
3. Press your preferred key combination
4. Click **Save**

## 3. Your First Dictation

1. Open any app — Notes, ChatGPT, Gmail, VS Code
2. Place your cursor where you want text
3. Press and hold your hotkey
4. Speak naturally
5. Release the hotkey — text appears where your cursor was

## Tips

- **Pause punctuation**: Say "comma", "period", "new line" — VoiceTypr inserts them
- **Numbers**: Say "one twenty three" or "one hundred and fifty"
- ** corrections**: Just backspace and re-dictate. There's no cloud delay.
