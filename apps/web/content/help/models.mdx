Voicetypr runs speech recognition entirely on your device using open-source Whisper models.

## Model Comparison

| Model | Size | Speed | Accuracy | Best For |
|-------|------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Whisper Base** | ~140 MB | Fastest | Good | Clear speech, fast workflows |
| **Whisper Large v3** | ~2.9 GB | Slower | Best | Noisy environments, accents |
| **Whisper Large v3 Turbo** | ~1.5 GB | Fast | Very good | Balance of speed and accuracy |
| **Parakeet** (Apple Silicon) | ~0.6 GB | Fast | Very good | Mac users, real-time use |

## RAM Requirements

| Model | Minimum RAM | Recommended |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| Whisper Base | 4 GB | 8 GB |
| Whisper Large v3 Turbo | 8 GB | 16 GB |
| Whisper Large v3 | 16 GB | 32 GB |
| Parakeet | 4 GB | 8 GB |

## Language Support

All Whisper models support 99+ languages including:

English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, Polish, Japanese, Mandarin, Hindi, Arabic, Korean, Turkish, Russian, and 85 more.

Parakeet is optimized for English but supports other languages with varying accuracy.

## Switching Models

1. Open Voicetypr settings
2. Go to **Model**
3. Select a different model
4. Download if needed (one-time)
5. Restart Voicetypr

## AI Formatting

Voicetypr can reformat your dictation with AI presets:

- **Default** — Clean punctuation and spacing
- **Prompts** — Optimized for AI chat (ChatGPT, Claude)
- **Email** — Professional email formatting
- **Commit** — Git commit message style

These run using your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, or a custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint).
